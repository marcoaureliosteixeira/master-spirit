## SEGMENTO 1 — PARTIDA DE URANTIA (4s)
Ref: Documento 15 — Os Sete Superuniversos

VISUAL: Terra (Urantia) vista do espaço. Fio de luz dourada emerge da superfície — alma iniciando ascensão. Câmera se afasta. Despedida serena.

PROMPT:
Cinematic slow pullback from planet Earth seen from low orbit. The planet is vivid blue with white cloud swirls, thin atmospheric glow on the horizon. A single thread of warm golden light energy rises gently from the planet's surface upward into the cosmos, like a soul beginning its ascension. Camera pulls back smoothly and steadily, Earth growing smaller against the vast black of deep space. Stars begin to appear in the background. No human figures. Extremely photorealistic, NASA-quality Earth rendering. Anamorphic lens flare. Color palette: deep ocean blue, atmospheric white, golden light thread, black space. Shot inspired by Interstellar's Earth departure sequence. 4K, 24fps, cinematic color grade, film grain. Deeply emotional, serene, contemplative. The feeling of gratitude and departure.

## SEGMENTO 2 — OS MUNDOS DAS MANSÕES (4s)
Ref: Documento 47 — Os Sete Mundos das Mansões

VISUAL: 7 esferas translúcidas cristalinas. Tons azul-gelo a dourado. Estruturas de luz dentro (arcos, torres, jardins). Neblina luminosa conectando as esferas.

PROMPT:
Seven translucent luminous spheres floating in deep space in a gentle orbital formation. Each sphere has a slightly different color temperature -- ranging from ice blue to soft violet to pale gold -- all with a crystalline, light-made-solid quality. Inside the spheres, barely visible through the translucent surface: delicate crystalline structures, arched bridges of light, garden-like formations glowing softly. A luminous mist connects all seven spheres like energy veins or neural pathways. Camera glides slowly between and around the spheres, revealing their impossible beauty. Volumetric light rays pass through the translucent surfaces creating prismatic refractions. No human figures. Deep space background with distant star clusters. Inspired by the crystalline worlds of Tree of Life's cosmic sequence. 4K anamorphic, cinematic color grade. Color palette: ice blue, soft violet, pale gold, crystalline white, deep space black. Ethereal, sacred, breathtaking. The feeling of arriving somewhere that always existed in dreams.

## SEGMENTO 3 — UNIVERSO LOCAL DE NEBADON (4s)
Ref: Documento 43 — As Constelações (Edentia)

VISUAL: Paisagem cósmica vasta. Edentia no centro — mundo-jardim com vegetação luminosa, árvores cristalinas, rios de energia. Centenas de mundos orbitando. Nebulosas como cortinas de seda.

PROMPT:
Vast cosmic landscape revealing a local universe with thousands of star systems. At the center, a magnificent garden-world sphere glowing with bioluminescent vegetation -- luminous flower fields, crystalline trees, rivers of liquid light energy flowing across its surface. The sphere radiates warm golden-green light. Hundreds of smaller architectural spheres orbit around it in perfect geometric patterns, connected by faint light bridges. Colorful nebulae in deep purple, teal, and rose frame the scene like cosmic silk curtains. The camera slowly pushes forward into this cosmic vista, conveying overwhelming scale -- worlds within worlds within worlds. No human figures. Ultra-detailed, photorealistic cosmic rendering. Inspired by Interstellar's wormhole approach and Tree of Life's creation sequence. 4K anamorphic, cinematic. Color palette: golden green, deep purple nebula, teal, warm gold, rose. Sacred vertigo. The feeling of realizing the universe is infinitely larger than imagined.

## SEGMENTO 4 — SUPERUNIVERSO DE ORVONTON (4s)
Ref: Documento 15 — Os Sete Superuniversos

VISUAL: Via Láctea em toda glória. Bilhões de estrelas em espiral. Câmera percorre braço espiral como rio de luz. Pontos de luz intensa marcam sedes cósmicas. Nas bordas, outras estruturas galácticas (1/7 do todo).

PROMPT:
The Milky Way galaxy seen from above in its full spiral glory, slowly rotating. Billions of stars creating rivers of light along spiral arms. Golden cosmic dust, violet and deep blue gas clouds weaving between star clusters. The camera travels along one spiral arm like flying through a river of starlight, revealing the overwhelming scale -- hundreds of billions of stars. Scattered throughout, points of intensely bright concentrated light mark cosmic headquarters locations. The entire galaxy is framed as just ONE SEVENTH of something even larger -- subtle hints of other galactic structures visible at the extreme edges of frame. No human figures. Ultra-photorealistic astrophotography quality, Hubble/James Webb telescope aesthetic. 4K anamorphic, cinematic lens. Color palette: golden starlight, deep violet, cobalt blue, white-hot star clusters, cosmic dust amber. Inspired by Interstellar's Gargantua approach and Voyage of Time's cosmic sequences. Overwhelming beauty. The feeling of being a grain of dust contemplating infinity -- and belonging to it.

## SEGMENTO 5 — HAVONA E A ILHA DO PARAÍSO (8-10s) ★ CLÍMAX
Ref: Documentos 11-14 — A Ilha do Paraíso e o Universo Central

VISUAL: 1 bilhão de mundos perfeitos em 7 circuitos concêntricos. Cores: safira → violeta → esmeralda → turquesa → dourado → branco. Câmera mergulha pro centro. Ilha do Paraíso — superfície elíptica de LUZ PURA. Sem sombras, sem tempo. O fio dourado da Terra chega ao destino.

PROMPT (Parte A — 0-4s):
Camera crosses a boundary of pure white light and enters a vast cosmic structure -- one billion perfect worlds arranged in seven concentric orbital circuits around an absolute center. Each circuit glows with its own color intensity: outermost circuits in sapphire blue and deep violet, middle circuits in emerald and turquoise, innermost circuits in pure gold and incandescent white. The worlds are luminous spheres of impossible perfection, each one radiating sublime light. The concentric pattern creates hypnotic sacred geometry -- circles within circles, orbits within orbits. Camera pushes steadily toward the center, crossing through each circuit, each one brighter than the last. Volumetric light, lens flares increasing in intensity. 4K anamorphic cinematic.

PROMPT (Parte B — 4-10s):
Camera arrives at the absolute center -- the Isle of Paradise. Not a planet. Not a star. A vast elliptical surface of PURE LIGHT with no shadows, no darkness, no boundaries visible. The light does not illuminate things -- it IS everything. All energy in the universe emanates from this point. The light is warm, golden-white, infinite, overwhelming yet peaceful. The single golden thread of light from the first segment arrives here, merging into the infinite light source. Everything converges. Slow camera push into the light, becoming progressively more abstract, more luminous, until the entire frame is pure warm golden-white radiance. The most beautiful, most emotional moment. Inspired by the stargate sequence in 2001: A Space Odyssey combined with the light of Tree of Life's grace sequences. Color palette transitions: sapphire blue > violet > emerald > turquoise > gold > incandescent white > pure warm light. No human figures. Sacred. Transcendent. The feeling of arriving HOME after the longest journey. Pure love made visible.
