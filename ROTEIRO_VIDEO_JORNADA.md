# ROTEIRO VISUAL — "A Jornada"
## Vídeo Cinematográfico Master Spirit (30-45 segundos)
## Direção: Wagner de Assis

---

## CONCEITO

Jornada de CONSCIÊNCIA (não morte) — da Terra até a presença de Deus.
Expansão espiritual passando por esferas cada vez mais luminosas.
Referências: mundos morontiais (Urantia), colônias espirituais (André Luiz).

---

## CENA 1 — A PARTIDA (0s-6s)

VISUAL: Terra vista do espaço, linha entre dia e noite. Câmera se afasta lentamente. Trilha de partículas douradas acompanha — energia de consciência se expandindo.
CORES: Azul profundo, branco, dourado suave.
CÂMERA: Pull-back lento.
MOOD: Serenidade, contemplação.

---

## CENA 2 — SISTEMA SOLAR (6s-12s)

VISUAL: Câmera acelera. Lua, Marte, anéis de Saturno brilhando como cristal. Estrelas se esticam em linhas de luz. Espaço muda de azul-escuro pra púrpura e violeta.
CÂMERA: Travelling frontal acelerando.
MOOD: Exaltação, transcendência.

---

## CENA 3 — ESFERAS ESPIRITUAIS (12s-20s)

VISUAL: Nebulosas vivas pulsantes. Esferas translúcidas de cristal vivo e luz condensada. Tons turquesa, rosa-dourado, lavanda iridescente. Brevemente: cidades de luz — torres translúcidas, jardins de claridade. Cada esfera mais luminosa que a anterior.
CÂMERA: Passagem rente às esferas.
MOOD: Fascínio, descoberta, elevação.

---

## CENA 4 — GRANDE TRAVESSIA (20s-30s)

VISUAL: 7 níveis de esferas em sequência:
- Externas: azul e verde (materialidade sutil)
- Intermediárias: dourado e âmbar (espiritualidade)
- Internas: branco e prata (proximidade com a Fonte)
Partículas de todas as cores convergem pro centro — vórtice de luz dourada crescendo.
CÂMERA: Velocidade vertiginosa, desacelerando.
MOOD: Clímax emocional, ser chamado pelo amor.

---

## CENA 5 — A PRESENÇA (30s-40s)

VISUAL: Vórtice se abre como flor cósmica — pétalas de energia branca e dourada. SILÊNCIO. Luminosidade infinita, quente, acolhedora. Pulsa como respiração universal. Branco puro com dourado e rosa suave. Partículas de todas as esferas orbitam a Luz. Sem forma, sem rosto — apenas presença.
CÂMERA: Para. Contemplativa. 3-4 segundos de pura presença.
MOOD: Amor infinito, paz absoluta, lar espiritual.

---

## CENA 6 — MASTER SPIRIT (40s-45s)

VISUAL: Tela escurece pra dourado escuro. Logo MASTER SPIRIT em branco luminoso com partículas douradas. Fade out.

## PROMPTS PARA GERAÇÃO DE VÍDEO (IA)

### Prompt Completo (unificado)

A cinematic cosmic journey of consciousness traveling from Earth through spiritual spheres to the divine source of light. Shot in ultra-cinematic 4K style, anamorphic lens, film grain, inspired by Terrence Malick's "The Tree of Life", Christopher Nolan's "Interstellar", and Stanley Kubrick's "2001: A Space Odyssey". Smooth continuous camera movement pulling forward through space.

The journey begins with Earth seen from orbit at the terminator line between day and night, blue oceans, white clouds, golden sunlight on the atmosphere edge. Camera slowly pulls back as a trail of golden light particles emerges and moves away from Earth. The camera accelerates through the solar system, passing Saturn's rings glowing like crystal halos, stars stretching into light streaks as speed increases.

The dark void of space gradually transforms into deep purple and violet hues. Luminous translucent spheres appear — worlds made of condensed light and crystalline energy, glowing in turquoise, rose-gold, and iridescent lavender. Brief glimpse of translucent city-like structures of pure light on one sphere surface. Each successive sphere is brighter, more radiant.

Seven concentric spherical realms pass in sequence — outer ones in blue-green tones, middle ones in golden amber, inner ones in silver-white — all orbiting a growing central point of golden light. Particles of every color converge toward this center.

The camera decelerates. The golden vortex opens like a cosmic flower — petals of white and gold energy unfold in all directions. Absolute stillness. An infinite warm white light with subtle gold and soft pink hues fills the entire frame — pulsing gently like a universal breath. Tiny luminous particles from all traversed spheres orbit this central light source. Pure love, pure presence, pure warmth.

Cinematic style: volumetric god rays, lens flares, particle systems, nebula clouds, ethereal glow, hyperspace light trails. Color palette progression: deep blue to purple to turquoise-gold to pure radiant white-gold. Camera: continuous forward tracking shot, smooth deceleration at climax. Mood: awe-inspiring, emotionally overwhelming, spiritually transcendent, deeply peaceful. No human figures. No text. Pure cosmic visual poetry.

### Segmento 1 — A Partida (4s)

Cinematic 4K shot of planet Earth seen from low orbit, terminator line between day and night, blue oceans and white clouds, golden sunlight glowing on the atmosphere edge. Camera slowly pulls back into space. A delicate trail of golden luminous particles streams outward from Earth into the cosmos. Deep blue and black space background with distant stars. Anamorphic lens, film grain, volumetric atmospheric glow. Mood: serene, contemplative, majestic. Inspired by Interstellar Earth departure scene. Smooth continuous backward tracking shot. No human figures.

---

### Segmento 2 — Sistema Solar (5-8s)

Cinematic 4K hyperspace travel through the solar system. Camera moves forward at accelerating speed past Saturn with its rings glowing like crystalline halos of light. Stars in the background stretch into long light streaks indicating immense velocity. The color of space gradually shifts from deep blue-black to deep purple and violet hues, as if the fabric of space itself is transforming. Lens flares, motion blur on star trails, anamorphic cinematography. Mood: exhilarating, transcendent acceleration. Inspired by Interstellar wormhole sequence. Continuous forward tracking shot gaining speed. No human figures.

---

### Segmento 3 — Esferas Espirituais (8-10s)

Cinematic 4K continuous forward flight through an ethereal cosmic realm. Multiple translucent luminous spheres float in space — worlds made of crystalline condensed light, glowing in turquoise, rose-gold, iridescent lavender, and pearl white. Camera flies close to one sphere revealing brief glimpse of translucent city-like structures made entirely of light — glowing towers, luminous gardens in celestial blue and white-gold tones. Nebula clouds in pink and gold surround the spheres. Volumetric god rays pierce through translucent surfaces. Each sphere passed is progressively brighter and more radiant than the last. Ethereal particle systems float everywhere. Mood: wonder, beauty, spiritual elevation. Inspired by Tree of Life creation sequence. Smooth forward tracking shot. No human figures.

---

### Segmento 4 — Travessia Final (6-8s)

Cinematic 4K dramatic forward flight through seven concentric luminous spherical realms arranged in cosmic orbital pattern. Outer spheres glow in blue-green energy, middle spheres radiate golden amber light, innermost spheres shine in brilliant silver-white. All spheres orbit around a massive growing central point of intense golden light — a vortex pulling everything toward it. Streams of multicolored light particles from all directions converge toward the golden center. Speed is immense. Emotional crescendo. Volumetric rays, lens flares, particle convergence, nebula wisps. Color palette shifts from cool blues through warm golds to brilliant white. Mood: overwhelming awe, spiritual climax, being called toward something infinite. Inspired by 2001 Space Odyssey stargate and Interstellar tesseract approach. Forward tracking shot at high speed with subtle deceleration. No human figures.

---

### Segmento 5 — A Presença de Deus (8-10s)

Cinematic 4K transcendent climax. Camera decelerates to stillness. A massive vortex of golden-white light slowly opens like a cosmic flower — enormous petals of pure white and gold energy gracefully unfold in all directions. The entire frame fills with infinite warm radiant light — pure white with subtle golden and very soft pink luminous hues. The light pulses gently and slowly like a universal breath, like a cosmic heartbeat of pure love. Tiny luminous particles of every color orbit peacefully within this infinite light source — remnants of all the spheres traversed, as if all creation is an emanation of this single source. Absolute stillness. Profound peace. Volumetric god rays emanating from center, soft ethereal glow, gentle lens bloom. Camera holds perfectly still in contemplation. Mood: infinite love, overwhelming peace, divine presence, spiritual homecoming. The most beautiful and emotionally moving image imaginable. Inspired by Tree of Life divine light sequences. Static contemplative frame. No human figures, no face, no form — only pure transcendent light and love.

---

### Segmento 6 — Logo (3-4s)

Cinematic 4K elegant fade from radiant golden-white divine light to a warm deep gold background like sunset seen from within divine light. Gentle luminous golden particles float and drift slowly across frame. Soft ethereal atmosphere. Mood: peaceful afterglow, sacred silence, gentle conclusion. Volumetric warm light, subtle bokeh particles, film grain. Static or very slow subtle camera movement. No text, no human figures. Clean minimal cosmic background for logo overlay.
